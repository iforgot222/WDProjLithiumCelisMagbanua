Art Themes: Religious

Religious and Biblical Art

This art theme was characterized by realistic, humanized figures in pivotal moments in the Bible. Biblical art features religious figures (in Christianity) like Mary, Jesus, Saints and was one of the most common themes in Renaissance, despite the secularization of society during the time.Example: The Last Supper by Leonardo Da Vinci.
